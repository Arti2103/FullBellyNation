ABOUT US

Our aim is to collect the surplus food at various auspicious occasions like parties,weddings,events and utilise the leftover food to donate among those in need.

Keeping the spirit of 'Annapurna' in mind people can prevent the food wastage by simply logging in our website. Through our advocacy work, we aim to raise awareness about the issue of hunger and push for policies and programs that address its root causes. We also support local and international organizations that work to provide food assistance, agricultural support, and education on nutrition and food security.
